What started as an idea by a group of developers from Edmonton, Canada and the Dev Edmonton Society has flourished into a truly global initiative, with participants from several countries across the globe.

CODEVID-19 is bringing together problem-solvers to build solutions for managing, understanding, and surviving the COVID-19 pandemic. Anyone interested in helping out is invited to join the fight and get involved.

    Developers, Designers, & Managers

    Becoming a Participant

    We have several teams working on groundbreaking projects and would love to have you be a part of it. Whether you have a project in mind or are looking for a team to tinker with, there are a few things you must do to participate.

    1. Read and abide by our Code of Conduct & Rules.
    2. Sign up at FindCollabs and browse through the projects. Join one or create your own.
    3. Join our Slack to interact with other participants, mentors, community ambassadors, and organizers.
      • #announcements: for the latest hackathon news
      • #looking-for-a-team: fill a skill gap or find a team to belong to
      • #brainstorming: discuss ideas for projects
      • #code-help: StackOverflow, CODEVID-19 style
      • #questions-for-organizers: we'll do our best to help!
    4. Getting stuck? Take a look at our available Resources and Blog for inspiration.

    Becoming a Mentor

    Want to help teams out but don't have a lot of time? Becoming a mentor would be the perfect solution for you!

    1. Read and abide by our Code of Conduct & Rules.
    2. Sign up at FindCollabs and browse through the projects. Join one or create your own.
    3. Join our Slack to interact with other participants, mentors, community ambassadors, and organizers.
      • #announcements: for the latest hackathon news
      • #brainstorming: discuss ideas for projects
      • #code-help: this is where you'll help the most!
      • #mentors: showcase your talents here
